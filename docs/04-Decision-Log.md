\## 2026-07-21



\### Decision



Users always have complete control over partner selection.



AI will assist users by recommending compatible profiles but will never replace personal choice.



\### Reason



SoulMatch believes that choosing a life partner is a deeply personal decision.



Artificial Intelligence should simplify the search process, explain compatibility, and save time while respecting the user's preferences and final decision.



\### Product Principle



\*\*You Choose. AI Helps.\*\*



Decision:

SoulMatch supports full-language switching instead of displaying two languages together.



Reason:

A single-language interface is cleaner, easier to read, and provides a better user experience. Users can switch the entire application between supported languages, and their preference is remembered for future visits.
