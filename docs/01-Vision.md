\## 1. Introduction



SoulMatch is a modern AI-powered matrimonial platform inspired by the timeless values of trust, respect, and lifelong companionship.



The platform begins by serving the Mithila community and is designed to expand across Bihar, India, and eventually to users around the world.



\---



\## 2. Mission



To provide a secure, trustworthy, and modern platform where individuals and families can confidently search for a life partner while respecting Indian values and embracing modern technology.



\---



\## 3. Vision



To become India's most trusted matrimonial platform by combining technology, security, simplicity, and cultural understanding.
